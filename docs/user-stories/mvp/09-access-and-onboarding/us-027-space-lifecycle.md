# US-027: Enforce Space Lifecycle

**Status:** Partial<br>
**Priority:** Must<br>
**OpenSpec:** Not created<br>
**Depends on:** Google authentication

## User Story

As a Leonly user, I want creation and invitation states to behave predictably so I always enter the correct private space without exceeding its membership limits.

## Intended Outcome

An authenticated user can create one active space or join one space by valid invite. Creating a space completes onboarding and opens the one-member dashboard; joining activates the two-member experience.

## Business Rules

- A user may have at most one active membership and a space at most two active members. Database transactions and constraints enforce both rules during concurrent joins.
- Creating a space creates its first active membership. The stored creator/owner attribute is informational and grants no extra authorization after creation.
- An invite expires 24 hours after issue and becomes invalid when the second member joins.
- While the space has one member, its creator can regenerate an expired or missing invite. Regeneration invalidates any prior code.
- A user cannot redeem their own invite, join the same space twice, or join while actively assigned to another space.
- Invalid, expired, already-used, full-space, and unauthorized invite attempts return safe errors without exposing space data.
- A user without an active membership enters create/join setup. A one-member creator has completed onboarding and enters the dashboard.
- Leaving, member removal, space closure, ownership transfer, and account deletion are outside MVP.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Create-space ends onboarding and routes the creator to the one-member dashboard.
- [ ] A valid invite atomically creates the second membership and cannot admit a third member.
- [ ] Concurrent redemption of the final slot admits exactly one eligible user.
- [ ] Expired invites cannot be redeemed and can be regenerated by the sole active member.
- [ ] Self-join, duplicate join, existing-active-space, invalid-code, and full-space attempts are rejected safely.
- [ ] Post-login routing distinguishes no-space setup, one-member waiting, and two-member active states.

## Verification

- [ ] Test creation, successful join, 24-hour expiry boundary, regeneration, and all invalid join cases.
- [ ] Test simultaneous final-slot redemption against database-enforced membership limits.
